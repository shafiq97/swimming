FAQ

-Who can register Malaysia Swimming Federation? This event open to all Malaysian.

-I have a problem with my registration. What should I do? Please screenshot the error message and email to info@swimming.asia, we will help and rectify the problem as soon as possible

- I have a problem with my record/result submission. What should I do? Please makesure screenshot the error message and email to info@swimming.asia together with your participant id and participant name that you registered, we will revert to you within 24 hours.
